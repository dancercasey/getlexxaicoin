How to Buy LEXX

  1. Open StellarTerm.
  2. Log in with your Stellar wallet (Lobstr, Freighter, or other supported wallets).
  3. Search for LEXX using the issuing account:
    GDN52ZWPZ54SB62RADM2ERMFG6CICOI53GA7PICZFOWLXIYRCZQIOYYS
